# Tutorial: Read Your First Analyst Card

This tutorial walks you through opening the Analyst panel and reading a single core-company card. By the end you will know where the report text comes from, where the portfolio weight comes from, and why the latest close appears next to the valuation paragraphs.

The Analyst panel shows 8 company research reports and 3 industry-chain summaries from your local `researcher/` directory, overlaid with the same day's portfolio data. It is read-only: it never modifies your holdings.

## What you'll need

- PortfolioM backend running on `http://localhost:8001`
- Frontend running on `http://localhost:5173`
- The `researcher/` directory at the repo root, containing 8 DOCX company reports and 6 Markdown chain files
- A business date with data already ingested (the page shows the most recent business date)

## Step 1: Open the Analyst tab

In the sidebar, click **分析师** (Analyst). You will see two sub-tabs: **核心公司** and **产业链**.

The first time you open it, if the page says "暂无核心公司研究报告", the `analyst_*` tables are empty — go to [How to ingest analyst data](./howto-analyst-restock-data.md) and run the ingest endpoint. Assume it is already loaded for the rest of this tutorial.

## Step 2: Read a folded card

Each company card has two visual states. Folded state shows the at-a-glance numbers:

```
688041.SH     海光信息               ▸
组合权重 2.05%    PE 38.6      PB 5.6
PS 12.3          最新收盘 95.40 (2026-06-20)
```

What you are looking at:

- **Stock code + name** comes from the filename (`688041.SH公司研究框架.docx`) and the first paragraph of the DOCX (regex `^(.{2,20})[（(]688041.SH[）)][:：]`). If the regex misses, `SecurityMaster` is the fallback.
- **组合权重** is the current portfolio weight of this stock on the latest business date. The denominator is the **total portfolio amount** (not the drilled-only amount).
- **PE / PB / PS** come from `AShareFinancialSnapshot` (or `HKShareFinancialSnapshot` for `.HK` codes) on that business date, preferring the dynamic `_dynamic` value, falling back to the static one.
- **最新收盘** is the `current_price` from the same snapshot, with its `current_price_date`. If the snapshot has no price, it falls back to the most recent row in `PriceCache`.

If a card shows `未持仓`, the stock is in the research catalog but not in your portfolio on that date. The PE/PB/PS still appear because they come from a snapshot, independent of holdings.

## Step 3: Expand the card

Click anywhere on the card header. Two tabs appear: **具体持仓** and **研究报告**.

**具体持仓** shows the "来源基金（约当数量口径）" table. Each row is a fund that holds this stock through drilling — meaning the fund is in `PenetrationSnapshot` for this stock on this date.

| 基金代码 | 基金名称 | 约当数量 | 金额(CNY) | 占组合% | 占基金% |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 007818.OF | 国泰中证全指通信设备ETF联接C | 69 | 94,143 | 1.60% | 22.27% |

- **约当数量** = `amount_cny_dynamic / current_price`. This is the share-equivalent that you indirectly own via this fund.
- **金额(CNY)** = `amount_cny_dynamic`, the dynamic CNY value of that share-equivalent.
- **占组合%** = `amount_cny_dynamic / total_portfolio_amount`.
- **占基金%** = `amount_cny_dynamic / holding_amount_cny` (the fund's total CNY holding amount for that business date).

Rows are sorted by `amount_cny_dynamic` descending. The total of `占组合%` across all source funds of one stock equals the **组合权重** shown on the folded card.

**研究报告** opens a 6-section pager with arrows. The five sections are extracted from the DOCX by matching heading text against the section keywords defined in `services/analyst_parser.py`:

| Pager label | Source field |
|---|---|
| 一、市场关注 | `section_1_market_focus` |
| 二、核心竞争力 | `section_2_core_competence` |
| 三、供需格局 | `section_3_supply_demand` |
| 四、边际变化 | `section_4_marginal_change` |
| 五、估值 | `section_5_valuation` |
| 六、风险 / Alpha | `section_6_risk` |

If the report's first 20 characters inside parentheses match the stock code, the company name is auto-extracted. Otherwise the `SecurityMaster` table fills it in.

In the **五、估值** section, every occurrence of `数字+元` is followed by a small gray badge showing the actual closing price on the snapshot date — for example `目标价 120 元 close 95.40 (2026-06-20)`. This badge is generated by `injectPriceBadges` on the front-end and does not modify the report text.

## What you built

You now know:

- Where the company name, weight, PE/PB/PS, and latest close come from on the folded card.
- How to read a source-fund row in the expanded holding tab.
- How the six report sections are mapped from DOCX headings to the pager.
- Why a "close 95.40" badge appears next to target prices in the valuation section.
